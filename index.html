About the Author:

R. L. Laster began implementing exegesis and hermeneutics at 27 years of age. Although Laster grew up as a prominent musician for Pentecostal worship services, he pushed through religious convictions to dive deep into theology, its original transcripts and their insights. Laster insists that inner-alchemy (the transmutation of consciousness) is vital to man-kinds spiritual prosperity. His novel series INEFFABLE is his latest project for assisting in such an endeavor. He can usually be found contributing to online forums about matters of existentialism, the expansion of consciousness, spirit-sciences, esoteric mysticism and parapsychology.When He's not spending time at his writer’s desk, he's delighting himself under the tutelage of spiritual leaders, practicing meditations, lucid dreaming, antiquing or playing piano. Laster resides in Las Vegas, Nevada.

“Albeit a challenging task, writing a character-based novel series about the divine feminine and the women’s god-like capacities have always been one of my deepest desires.” - R. L. Laster

SERIES BLURB:

Michelle (main character) is a natural-birthed, wide-eyed, Hawaiian delight. As a dancing toddler she hosts a strong frequency and sublime talent for observation. Though Michelle began uttering ancient wisdoms, (oddly) she slowly commits to selective mute’ism by age 5. As a teenager, Michelle (SHORTY) and her best friend from the SAN DIEGO DANCE ACADEMY dance their way onto a world-recognized stage. The syndicated performance is where the speechless teen debuts god-like powers unlike anything the modern world has ever witnessed. Michelle's troop reaches 4 young ladies as they perform dance routines and end their sets assisting Michelle's demonstrations that baffle and defy physics and reality. Shorty champions the unleashed power of the feminine divine. The miraculous one starts an all-female sorority of spirit sciences Family of Light Kindred Sororities - or "F.O.L.K.S." for short. From raising the dead; to galactic flight, the governments (and the Church) are forced to take notice and consider see her as a threat to traditional religion and established (deep state) agenda. In efforts to control the miraculous, Michelle proves to be much more-- immortal-- a god. Michelle is INEFFABLE. Prepare for an emotionally-charged thrill ride of enlightenment.
